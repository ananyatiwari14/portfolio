ABOUT

I am an award-winning journalist with a track record of impactful reporting across news beats. I have experience in investigative journalism, data analysis, and multimedia storytelling. I am adept at breaking exclusive stories, providing insightful coverage, and thriving under tight deadlines.

I hold a Master of Journalism & Digital Media degree from the University of California, Berkeley, which I obtained in 2023. Prior to that, I completed both a Master of Arts and a Bachelor of Arts in English Literature at the prestigious University of Delhi in India. My educational background has provided me with a strong foundation in journalism, digital media, and in-depth research.
